About me

I believe healing should feel human

Hi, I'm Dr. Shruti — a physiotherapist who built this practice around one simple idea: that every person deserves care that truly listens to them.

I started this journey because I saw how impersonal healthcare had become. Rushed appointments, generic advice, and little follow-through. I wanted to do things differently — slower, more thoughtful, more personal.

Today, I work one-on-one with people who are ready to feel better. Whether you're dealing with chronic pain, recovering from an injury, or just noticing your body isn't moving the way it used to — I'm here for all of it.

I come to you, or you come to me. Either way, every session is tailored entirely to you — because your pain isn't textbook, and your recovery shouldn't be either.
